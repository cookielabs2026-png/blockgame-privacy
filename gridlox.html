Privacy Policy for Gridlox

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Gridlox ("the app", "we", "us") is a free puzzle game published by Cookie Labs. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information stored on your device

Your game progress — scores, level, coins, power-ups, unlocked board skins and settings — is stored locally on your own device. This data stays on your device, is not uploaded to us, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data. Gridlox does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, contacts, or photos.

Advertising

Gridlox is free and supported by ads. We use Google AdMob to display banner and rewarded advertisements. To serve and measure ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain information, including a device advertising identifier, IP address, and app-usage/interaction data. This information is handled under Google's policies. You can learn more, and manage ad personalization, here: policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Players who purchase the optional "Remove Ads" subscription will no longer be shown ads.

In-app purchases

Gridlox offers optional in-app purchases (coin packs and an ad-removal subscription). Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing; we do not receive or store your payment details.

Children's privacy

Gridlox is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the ad services, please contact us and we will address it.

Data we collect directly

None. Cookie Labs does not itself collect, store, or share any personal data. Any data processing described above is performed by Google (AdMob / Play Billing) as part of providing ads and purchases.

Changes to this policy

If the app or its data practices change, this policy will be updated and the new version posted at this same address before the change takes effect.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the contact details listed on our Google Play Store listing for Gridlox.
