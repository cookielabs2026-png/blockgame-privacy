Privacy Policy for Ember & Anvil

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Ember & Anvil ("the app", "we", "us") is a free blacksmithing game published by Cookie Labs. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Data we collect

None. Ember & Anvil does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking software of any kind. It does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, contacts, photos, or location.

Information stored on your device

Your game progress — coin, reputation, tool upgrades, materials and bars, achievements, day and event state, and Dynasty perks — is stored locally on your own device in the app's private storage. This data stays on your device, is never uploaded to us, and is removed if you uninstall the app, clear its data, or use the in-game "Start over" option.

Advertising

Ember & Anvil contains no advertising. No advertising software is included in the app, and no advertising identifier is collected or used.

In-app purchases

Ember & Anvil contains no in-app purchases. No billing or payment software is included in the app, and we never receive or store payment details.

Network use

The game plays entirely offline and makes no network requests of its own. The app declares the standard internet permission because the application framework it is built on requires it, but the game does not send or receive any data.

Children's privacy

Ember & Anvil is intended for a general audience. Because the app collects no personal information from any user, it collects none from children either.

Changes to this policy

If the app or its data practices change, this policy will be updated and the new version posted at this same address before the change takes effect.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the contact details listed on our Google Play Store listing for Ember & Anvil.
